Choose Your Own Adventure Game (CYOAG)

Goldilocks & the Three Bears

Goldilocks rolls up her sleeves, stands up straight, and looks the bears squarely in the eye. "Your move" she says, but before the bears can respond, she attacks fiercely and remorselessly. It turns out that noone fights as dirty as an eight year old girl. After a half-hearted defence, the bears all turn tail and run deep into the woods, never to be seen again.

The End
