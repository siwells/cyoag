Choose Your Own Adventure Game (CYOAG)

Goldilocks & the Three Bears

Goldilocks walks deep into the wood. She walks so far that she can no longer hear the songbirds singing in the meadow near her home. After a while she spies a small house with tiny windows and a little crooked chimney.

What should Goldilocks do next?

  • Enter the house
  • Return home to play in the garden
